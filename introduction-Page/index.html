A FOOD WASTE SOLUTION WITH A POSITIVE IMPACT

Hunger’s Heroes app connects restaurants with surplus food to charitable organizations helping communities facing food insecurities. With a tap of a finger you can increase food supply, eliminate food waste, and help the planet.

Donate Food

Restaurants, fast food chains with food donations can list the items on the app, request a pickup, notify local charitable organizations.

Recieve Food

Nonprofits can be notified by the restaurant when donation is available and receive high-quality donations.

HOW HUNGER’S HEROES WORKS
